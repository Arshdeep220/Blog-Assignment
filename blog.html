Alex Nuńez

art & design is my passion

Color, glitter, feathers, Cynthia the doll from Rugrats: all of these are present in works by Miami-based painter Alex Nuńez. The titles are good, too. There's Put some lipstick on and pull yourself together and Don't set yourself on fire to keep others warm.

Nunez displays in her work a deep understanding of what it really feels like to be a woman today — but unlike the rest of us, who settle for tweeting something salty or complaining to our roommate, she channels that into orgiastic explosions of color you can't help but smile at.
